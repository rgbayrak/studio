Education

I am a self-taught artist. To me, that means I get to choose my own journey — deciding what to learn, who to learn from, and when to go deeper. This page is a record of that journey: the instructors, courses, and workshops that have shaped my practice. It is not a credential list — it is a map of curiosity.
